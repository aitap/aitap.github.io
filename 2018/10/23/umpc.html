Modern Ultra-mobile PCs

The market of sub-11" laptops, also known as UMPCs, seems almost non-existent nowadays, but it is not completely devoid of life. Moreover, 2018 saw the emergence of new UMPC models, most of them produced in China. Here I will try to summarize the available spectrum of modern models:

  • GPD Pocket
  • GPD Pocket 2
  • GPD Win 2 (there also was GPD Win 1, but it doesn't seem to be sold anymore)
  • One-Notebook OneMix Yoga
  • One-Notebook OneMix 2 Yoga (pre-order)
  • Gemini PDA
